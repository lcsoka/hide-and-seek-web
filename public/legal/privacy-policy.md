## 1. What we collect

- **Account data** (registered users): email address, a hashed password, display name, and an
  optional profile picture.
- **Guest data**: the display name you choose and a temporary account identifier. Guest accounts
  are automatically deleted after a period of inactivity.
- **Precise location data**: while you are in a game, your device's GPS coordinates are collected and
  stored as a position history so the game can run and be replayed. This is **precise geolocation**
  and is the most sensitive data we process.
- **Gameplay data**: your role, questions asked/answered, curses played, transit journeys, timings,
  scores, and any photos you upload as in-game proof.
- **User-generated content** (registered users): custom questions and curses you create.
- **Feedback** you submit through the app.
- **Technical data**: IP address and basic request logs, device/browser information, and
  authentication tokens needed to keep you signed in.

We do **not** knowingly collect data from children under the age required by [JURISDICTION]. We do
**not** use advertising or third-party analytics/tracking.

## 2. Why we use it, and our legal basis

| Purpose | Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6) |
|---|---|
| Run the game (location, gameplay, real-time features) | Performance of a contract; your **consent** for precise location |
| Create and secure your account | Performance of a contract |
| Show your match history and stats | Legitimate interest / contract |
| Prevent abuse, keep the service reliable | Legitimate interest |
| Respond to feedback and support requests | Legitimate interest |

You can withdraw location consent at any time by disabling location access or leaving the game;
gameplay that depends on location will then stop working.

## 3. Who we share it with (processors & recipients)

- **Hosting**: our servers run on **DigitalOcean** (data processor).
- **OpenStreetMap infrastructure**: to answer geographic questions and draw the map, coordinates and
  map queries are sent to **Overpass / Nominatim / OSM tile providers**. This means location-derived
  queries reach these third parties. See their respective privacy terms.
- **Email delivery**: password-reset and notification emails may be sent via **[EMAIL PROVIDER]**.

We do not sell your personal data. We only share what is necessary to operate the service.

## 5. How long we keep it

- **Guest accounts and their data**: deleted automatically after inactivity.
- **Finished or abandoned games** (including position tracks): deleted after **30 days**.
- **Registered accounts**: kept until you delete your account (see §7) or ask us to.
- **Request logs**: kept for a short period for security and debugging.

## 6. How we protect it

Passwords are stored only as salted hashes; connections use HTTPS/WSS; access to production data is
restricted. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable measures appropriate to the risk.

## 7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you may: access your data; correct it; **erase it**; restrict or object to processing;
receive a portable copy; and withdraw consent.

- **Delete your account yourself**: open **Profile → Danger zone → Delete my account**. This
  permanently erases your account, stats, and custom content, revokes your sessions, and anonymises
  your past game participation for other players. It cannot be undone.
- For any other request, contact **[CONTACT EMAIL]**.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Hungarian supervisory authority, the **National
Authority for Data Protection and Freedom of Information (NAIH)** — naih.hu.
